features

  • Type : Double Skinned
  • MOC Inner : Stainless Steel (SS-304)
  • MOC Outer : CRC Sheet Duly Powder Coated.
  • Temp. Controller : µ Processor Auto-Tune LED Display
  • Temperature Range : Ambient +5°C to 80°C
  • Accuracy : ±0.1°C
  • Electrical : 220/230 volts AC, 50/60 Hz
  • Styled and recessed side lifting handles facilitates ease of handling.
  • Double Skinned Construction,
  • Inner chamber made of Stainless Steel SS-304.
  • Outer wall made of CRC sheet duly powder coated.
  • Gap between inner & Outer chamber is filled with high grade insulation for minimum thermal loss.
  • Heating elements underneath the diffuser tray provides a “clear and “clutter free” working area and facilitates ease of cleaning.
  • Totally covered underside base provides for high speed energy (heat) transfer, faster heat up time thus resulting in reduction of energy consumption was improved uniformity which in turn speeds heat uniformity over a broader area.
  • Rugged, high-performance bath maintains water temperature from ambient +5°C to 80°C.
  • Fitted with non-slip rubber feet for safety.
  • Microprocessor Digital temperature readout with least count/resolution of 0.1°C
  • Clearly visible brights status indicators display set valve and process value simultaneously with provision for digital calibration through membrane keys.
  • Allows easy temperature setting with ±0.1°C precision at the push of a button.
  • Microprocessor control with bright three-digit LED displays process bath temperature as well as set value range with integrated timer.
  • Integrated minute timer allows starts automatically. Once the unit switched “ON” heating starts automatically and the set/desired temperature is achieved, the inbuilt AUTOMATIC DIGITAL MINUTE TIMER starts automatically. Once this set period is over the heaters are switched “OFF” automatically with Buzzer, alerting the user for cycle end.
  • Includes a stainless-steel flat lid.
  • Diffuser Tray: All systems come with a stainless steel perforated diffuser tray-the diffuser tray separates contents placed in to the water bath from the bottom of the tank to promote better temperature uniformity.
  • Independent high limit Over-temperature safety circuit prevents thermal overrun.
  • User friendly and easy to use controls simplify operation.
  • Drain outlet provided for fast emptying.
  • It has a seamless tank with rounded corner.
