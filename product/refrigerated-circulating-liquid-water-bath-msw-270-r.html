REFRIGERATED CIRCULATING LIQUID/(R)WATER BATH MSW-270(R)

This is a small description

Download Brochure
Enquire Now

applications

  • Most ideal for external temperature control applications to external systems such as Rotary Vacuum Evaporators, Refractometers, Polarimeters, Fermenters, Electrophoresis Chamber, Chromatography Columns, Condensers, Rheometers, and general laboratory cooling applications.

advantages & benefits

  • A compact refrigerated circulator.
  • Eliminates the hassles of using a separate Chiller.
  • Builts in circulator pump for efficient circulating processes.
  • It offers highest level of performance, flexibility and control for the most demanding applications.
  • Low noise level.
  • Wide operating ranges and applications.

constructional features

  • Inner bath chamber & lid made of Stainless Steel (SS-304 Grade).
  • Outer body made of CRC Sheet duly powder coated.
  • Space between inner and outer chamber filled with high grade PUF insulation for minimal thermal losses.
  • High Condensation efficiency.
  • Adjustable working temperature ranges available.
  • Environment friendly CFC FREE refrigerants and hermetically sealed compressor used.
  • Reduces tap water consumption thus reducing high cost of water and waste water.
  • Waste water does not contain hazardous substances thus eliminating danger to personnel and environment caused by solvents.

standard features

  • Various temperature ranges available to choose from depending upon varied requirements and applications.
  • Microprocessor based autotune PID TEMPERATURE CONTROLLER
  • Tighter cooling control for reduced energy consumptions.
  • Temperature control accuracy of ±0.5°C at -10°C.
  • Internal circulation enables precise temperature stability/uniformity in the bath better than ± 0.5°C. (at zero delta, at 37°C)
  • Temperature Display Resolution 0.1°C.
  • Powerful stirring circulation pump with 10-12 Lpm capacity at zero head for water.
  • Do not use water below 5°C (use ethanol below 5°C).
  • Do not use water above 90°C (use silicone oils etc above 90°C).
  • To work on 220/230 volts, 50/60 Hz AC supply.
