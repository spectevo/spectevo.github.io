ABOUT US

"Enabling Scientific community by Creating conditions"

Spectevo Technologies is registered as category “A” organization under the MSME act of The Govt. Of India & registered under Start-up India, under the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, Govt of India. We bring complete, functional, cutting-edge projects to fruition, with a focus on advanced technology (high-tech equipment, instruments, infrastructure), Safe environments (fire, containment zones) and high-quality environments (clean rooms).

Spectevo Technologies (Group of Companies) evolved from laboratory consumables & equipment trading to leading Pilot Plant/ Laboratory /BSL3/ABSL3 etc. Infrastructure Turnkey Solutions Provider cum system integrator in India. We engage in flexible aggregating service to suit the custom needs of various customers. We are a single point of contact from concept, design, execution, testing, and commissioning which is serviced by integrating high-quality and innovative products & equipment manufactured at different locations (Baroda, Hyderabad, and Ghaziabad), Reputed Principles and imports and ensure the completion of all documentation to comply with local authorities.

We are a System Integrator company (development, engineering, project delivery, and implementation) with an experienced group of technicians, technologists, engineers, and principals (Indian & Foreign) who manage and turn project ideas into reality. We offer our customers comprehensive services in project planning, manufacture, construction, and subsequent servicing. Our solutions guarantee the optimization of capital and operating costs and consistently emphasize protection of the environment. Within the framework of our services, we deliver our own Glass Reactors, Pilot Plants, Rotary Evaporators, Air Handling Units & clean Room equipment as well.

We provide fabrications for the pharmaceutical, biotechnological, healthcare, science and research, microelectronics, and automotive industries as well as for other industrial buildings utilizing cleanroom technology.
